Celebrating 70 Years of Excellence

Singapore Polytechnic celebrates its upcoming 70th anniversary with a new book chronicling its journey.

The book reflects on its journey and contribution to shaping polytechnic education in the region, alongside the other four institutions.

Singapore Polytechnic has gathered $2.6 million in donations to assist students requiring financial support.

In celebration of its 70th anniversary, Singapore Polytechnic (SP) will award annual bursaries of $2,500 each to over 100 students, funded by donations collected by the institution.

Singapore Polytechnic Students' Innovations Set to Enhance Boeing Aircraft Safety and Convenience

Boeing has patented three innovative features designed by Singapore Polytechnic students, including improved larder compartments and a locking system. These were developed during a 2013 collaboration and may be used in future aircraft.

Singapore Polytechnic unveils new food lab to foster innovation and collaboration.

SP’s new Future Food Lab, launched on April 17, showcases student innovations like a plant-based patty and a mushroom protein bar. The lab supports Singapore’s food security goals and partners with food tech companies to mentor students and advance sustainable food development.

Singapore Polytechnic celebrates its 70th anniversary as it awards diplomas to over 5,400 graduates

As Singapore Polytechnic celebrates its 70th anniversary, over 5,400 students graduated in its 64th ceremony. DPM Heng Swee Keat distributed diplomas and medals, highlighting the increasing importance of polytechnic education amid rapid innovation and job market changes.

SP 70th anniversary Music Festival

For SP’s 70th anniversary, a music festival was held on campus. Despite the rain, attendees remained enthusiastic, dancing to local artists like Evan Low, Rangga Jones, and Marian Carmel. The event emphasised the link between music and mental wellness, highlighting the importance of youth mental health.
